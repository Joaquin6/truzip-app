General Questions

We run targeted google/bing cpc campaigns in your city to generate leads (both buyer and seller). Our site is highly optimized to capture buyer and seller leads.

2. Are there any contracts, setup fees, cancellation fees.

No fees. Just pay $19 per lead. It is a monthly subscription - but cancel for free at any time.

3. Are the leads shared with other agents or are they exclusive?

The leads are exclusive to you. They are not shared with others.

4. How long will it take to generate the leads.

We start your campaign immediately and usually have a lead within 0-7 days. However, depending on demand, it could take upto 30 days.

5. Is the lead guaranteed to be in my city?

We try to generate the lead in your specified city, but the lead could be within a 15 mile radius of your city as well.

6. Can you guarantee the lead will convert?

We cannot. We do however guarantee that the lead will be valid and unique only to you.

7. If a lead inquires about several properties, is that considered 1 lead or multiple?

It is considered as 1 lead. A single unique buyer or seller may ask for information multiple times, but it is considered as 1 lead.

8. Can I choose whether I want only buyer or seller leads?

Not at this time. We will let you know if a lead is a buyer or seller though.
