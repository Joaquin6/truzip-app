FAQ

1. When I signup for Premium User, do I get access to ALL premium listings in California?

Yes. You get access to all premium listings in any zipcode, city or search area.

2. Why is it that my search area has no premium listings?

If your search parameters are very narrow (or there are very few listings), it is possible that there will be no premium listings. This is very unlikely though.

3. Once I make the payment, when do I get access?

Access is immediate. You will be given a username and password with which you login to view premium listings.

4. I'm having trouble accessing my account.

Please email us at support@truzip.com and we will resolve it asap.
