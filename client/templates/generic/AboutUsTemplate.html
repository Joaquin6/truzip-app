About Us

Who is Truzip?

I want to buy a home. How do I get started?

To get started, contact an agent in your area of interest. The agent will alert you on deals for free, with no obligation.

I want to sell a home. How do I get started?

Keep more of your equity when you work with a Truzip agent. Our top partner agents are incentivized to get you top dollar while charging only a 1.2% fee.
